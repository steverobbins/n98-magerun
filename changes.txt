--------------------------
REMOVED DEPRECATED ALIASES
--------------------------

We have removed all deprecated aliases.

- cache:clear
- database:console
- database:dump
- database:info
- system:modules:list
- system:cron:list
- system:setup:compare-versions
- sys:run-setup-scripts
- system:run-setup-scripts
- system:store:list
- system:url:list
- system:website:list
- system:check
- system:info
- system:maintenance

You can restore the old aliases by new "command alias feature" by adding
some entries in your configuration.

Please look in our wiki:

"https://github.com/netz98/n98-magerun/wiki/Command-aliases"
